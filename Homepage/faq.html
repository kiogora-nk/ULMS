Librarian FAQs

Here are some of the Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are the library's operating hours?
Our library is open Monday to Friday from 8 AM to 10 PM, Saturday from 10 AM to 5 PM, and closed on Sundays.
2. Incase I delay or fail to return a book in time are there any charges?
Overdue library books will be charged a fine of:
(a)Longloan-Ksh 5.00 per day.
(b)Shortloan-Ksh 5/= for the first hour and Sh.10 for every subsequent hour.
3.Am I allowed to photocopy a library book?
Photocopying is available at ksh.3/= per copy.Note: All photocopying is subject to :
(a) Copyright Act )Cap 130 Laws of Kenya)
(b)The Librarian?s authority to photocopy
(c) Written permission from the author's Theses and Dissertations.
4. When are students registered as library members?
Registration of students will be done within the first month after admission in the first year subject to undergoing orientation.
5. What would be the consequncies of loosing a library book.
For lost books members will be:
(a)Three times the cost of the book in print.
(b) Five times the cost of the book out of print.
6.How can I borrow a library book?
All registered library members are allowed to borrow books and will be expected to identify themselves at the time of borrowing.
7. Can I print or copy documents at the library?
Yes, we have printing and copying services available. Please check the pricing details at the front desk.
8. What should I do if I lost a library book?
If you lose a book, please report it to the library staff as soon as possible. We can help you understand the replacement process.
9. How can I become a member of the University library?
A Membership to the library is open to registered students and Staff. Academic and Non academic staff are to be recommended by their head of department.
10. How can I volunteer at the library?
We welcome volunteers! Please visit our website or contact the library to learn about current volunteer opportunities and how to apply.
